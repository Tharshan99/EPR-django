Our Interview Process
At EPR Groupers, we’ve designed a seamless and transparent 4-step process to ensure we find the perfect match for both you and our team. Showcase your skills, passion, and personality - we’re excited to meet the best version of you!

Step 1

Initial Screening
Our team will review your application and reach out for a quick chat to learn more about your background and aspirations.

Step 2

Technical Interview
Dive deeper into your expertise with a technical discussion led by our team leads. Show us what you’re made of!

Step 3

Practical Assessment
Tackle a real-world challenge designed to test your problem-solving skills and creativity. Let your work speak for itself.

Step 4

Final Evaluation
Meet with our senior leadership team for a comprehensive discussion about your fit within Rootcode’s culture and vision.
